Address to Government Regulatory and Standardization Bodies in Construction

To: Respected Leaders and Staff of Government Regulatory Agencies, Ministries, and Departments Responsible for Construction Safety!

This communication brings to your attention a systemic issue posing a direct threat to the lives and health of millions of people worldwide.

The issue concerns fire safety and the inadequacy of existing solutions for ensuring self-closing doors. Current building codes and standards mandate door closing to prevent the spread of smoke and fire. However, in practice, widely used mechanical door closers fail to provide the required reliability for several fundamental reasons:

1. Lack of 100% Reliability: Any mechanical door closer has a limited service life, is subject to wear, and can fail at the most critical moment, leading to door closer failure.
2. Vandalism and Theft: Closers are easy targets for vandals and thieves, compromising the entire building's building safety.
3. Incorrect Operation: Closer adjustments often get disrupted, leading to incomplete door closure, nullifying its fire safety function.
4. Lack of Safe Fixation Function: The vast majority of standard closers lack a built-in mechanism for safely holding a door open. Our proposed solution includes a magnetic holder-bumper that rotates 180°, providing reliable door retention. This is critical for evacuation, ventilation, or moving furniture.
5. High Opening Force: Overcoming the resistance of a powerful closer spring requires significant physical effort. This creates insurmountable barriers for children, the elderly, individuals with disabilities, and prevents pets (cats, dogs) from independently leaving a room in danger.

Current practices create an illusion of safety while failing to provide real protection. We are convinced it is time to revise outdated approaches and include innovative, non-mechanical methods in the registry of approved solutions, ensuring perpetual and absolute reliability.

We, the Virtual IQ Bio ecosystem, propose for inclusion in the regulatory framework the innovative methodology of the "Gravity Door Closer" ("Gravitsapa") — a method of installing a door block at a precise, calculated angle, causing the door to close under the force of gravity.

Key Safety Advantages:

· Evacuation Efficiency: The combination of the "Gravitsapa" and the magnetic holder drastically improves evacuation effectiveness. The door opens with minimal effort, and the ability to fix it open ensures unobstructed and safe egress for all, including mobility-impaired groups.
· Minimal Opening Force: Opening the door does not require compressing a powerful spring, needing several times less effort. The door becomes easy to open for all user groups and animals.
· Comprehensive Solution with Fixation: The technology includes a magnetic holder-bumper that ensures safe door fixation in the open position.
